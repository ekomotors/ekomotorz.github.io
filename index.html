motors

 Since incorporation in 2019,  ecomotors; our flagship company, has been driven by the vision of remaining timeless in relevance and value. By constantly keeping to our Brand promise, ekomotors has become a household name providing the Nigerian people with vehicles from a robustly diversified auto dealership, comprising of unlimited luxury and numerous value brands. These brands are showcased in our ultra modern showrooms nationwide. ekomotors has followed through by making significant investments in state of the art Aftersales facilities across the country and training of specialized personnel to ensure that our customers stay satisfied while putting their vehicles on the road. In addition, through strategic partnerships ekomotors ensures that wherever you are, our services are readily accessible.Asides from the brands that ekomotors represents, the company has also made it its duty to ensure that motorist are kept safe on the road and their vehicles in good condition by providing genuine automotive spare parts and automotive related products to the general public via distributors.
ekomotors sales and service locations are spread across the five geopolitical zones including Abuja, Kano, Gombe, Lagos, Ekiti, Ibadan, Port Harcourt, Uyo, Calabar, Enugu to mention a few.With a view to give back to our society, ekomotors regularly engages in social responsibility investments especially through the sponsorship of both public and private human development activities such as the annual week of caring, Public Private Auto Training Programmes at our auto training center amongst other...

mobility

..In a bid to cater for customers' mobility needs other than the purchase and service of luxury or value brand vehicles, ekomotors Mobility was incorporated in 2019 as a vessel for providing solution to consumer specific needs.At ekomotors, Mobility our services include but are not limited to car rentals (short & long term), leasing, logistics, haulage services, drivers & staff outsourcing and fleet management. It is the sole franchise operator of ekomotors Rent A Car in Nigeria. Through the supply and management of drivers and vehicles to private individuals, corporations and institutions alike, our top class fleet and rentals continuously provides a supportive, cost efficient means of transportation.Currently located in Lagos,and renders services nationwide; ekomotors Mobility aims to be an integral part of the day-to-day business processes of its clients making life easier as it supports your mobility requirements...
